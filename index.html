Our Story

From Humble Roots to
Global Presence

Founded in 2003 in New Delhi, Anjana International began as a modest textile trading firm serving markets in the Middle East and Southeast Asia. Over the years, our commitment to quality and ethical trade practices helped us expand into a multi-category export house operating across four continents.

Today, we represent hundreds of Indian manufacturers, artisans and producers — bringing the finest of India's industrial and artisanal output to buyers across Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific and the Gulf.

Our Standards

Quality is Not a
Process — It's a Culture

At Anjana International, quality assurance is embedded at every stage of our supply chain. From vendor evaluation and raw material inspection to pre-shipment audits and third-party testing, we ensure that every export meets the precise specifications of our buyers.

01

Vendor Qualification

All manufacturers undergo a rigorous 3-stage audit covering factory infrastructure, compliance documentation, and sample evaluation before onboarding.

02

In-Process Inspection

Our in-house QA team conducts inline inspections at 30%, 60% and 90% production stages to catch deviations early.

03

Pre-Shipment Audit

Final AQL 2.5 inspection as per ANSI/ASQ Z1.4 standards. Third-party inspection by SGS, Bureau Veritas or Intertek on request.

04

Laboratory Testing

NABL-accredited lab testing for physical, chemical and safety parameters as required by destination country regulations.
